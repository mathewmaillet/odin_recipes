Chicken Cordon Bleu

Description

Chicken breast stuffed with ham and swiss cheese. Covered in breading and baked.


Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Swiss Cheese
  • Thin sliced ham
  • Garlic
  • Egg
  • Breading
  • Herbs/Spices

Recipe

  1. Slice chicken breast open on one side.
  2. Fry up op Garlic, stuff chicken with Cheese and Ham.
  3. Add garlic. Whisk Egg and prepare flour and breading.
  4. Once stuffed dipped the chicken in the egg and then the flour, finally the breading.
  5. Let rest while the oven heats up.
  6. Bake until golden and crispy, then enjoy!
