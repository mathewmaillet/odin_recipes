LASAGNA

Description

Lasagna noodles covered in a meat sauce and way too much cheese

A great comfort food.


Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground protein
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Peppers (any color)
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Spinach
  • Herbs/Spices

Recipe

  1. Boil noodles until semi-soft. Do not overcook.
  2. Fry up garlic and onion, adding ground protein and cooking thoroughly.
  3. add tomato sauce to the protein mix
  4. layer noodles in a baking pan in a single layer.
  5. pour some of the protein/tomato sauce
  6. Add a thin layer of Ricotta cheese and spinach.
  7. Repeat the last 3 instructions until stacked as wanted.
  8. For the top layer add the Mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses liberally.
  9. Bake until cripsy and golden, then devour!
