Potato & Bacon Soup

Description

A creamy, savoury soup.


Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Bacon
  • Green Onions
  • Garlic
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Herbs/Spices

Recipe

  1. Dice potatoes and bacon into bitesized pieces
  2. Melt butter in a medium sized pot or pan
  3. finish dicing onions and garlic and to pot/pan
  4. add flour once fully melted. Mix well until thickened
  5. add onions and garlic.
  6. add milk and stir constantly until blended well
  7. add potatoes, onions, and bacon.
  8. simmer until potatoes are soft, and broth thickens.
  9. devour delicious soup
